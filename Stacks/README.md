6 functions are usually associated with Stacks:
- create(stack)
- isEmpty(stack)
- isFull(stack)
- pop(stack,x)
- peek(stack,num)

- Recursion and back tracking
- Recursion and Back Tracking
- Memory Management
- Balancing of parantheses
